Graph Isomorphism

A graph is a pair of a set of vertices and a set of edges among the vertices. It is commonly denoted $G=(V,E)$.

Two graphs $G$ and $H$ are isomorphic if there exists a permutation $$ p: V_G \rightarrow V_H $$ such that for all edges $\{v,u\} \in E_G,\ \{p(v),p(u)\} \in E_H $
